About Us

Pleasant Pool is a family-owned business that has been serving the community since 1985. We are dedicated to providing a safe and fun environment for all of our guests. Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and our staff is highly trained to ensure that you have the best experience possible. We offer a variety of services including swimming lessons, fitness classes, and a wide range of products to help you maintain a healthy lifestyle. We are committed to providing the highest quality service and we look forward to welcoming you to our facility.

Our Mission

We here at Pleasant Pool are dedicated to providing a safe and fun environment, while promoting healthy lifestyles. Come visit us for a swim, a fitness class, or to purchase products to help you maintain a healthy lifestyle. Nothing's too much trouble for our friendly, approachable team. We're here to help you make the most of your time at the pool.
